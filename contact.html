My name is Adrià Martínez Alegret, I am a graphic designer currently living in Reus, Catalonia. My interests range from graphic design, to the arts as a whole. I am also interested in games and web development.


As a kid, I always enjoyed creating stuff and playing video games, allowing my imagination to run wild. I quickly became interested in art and the fascinating world of design. After finishing secondary school, I studied art in high school and got a diploma. It was then, when I completely fell in love with graphic design.
